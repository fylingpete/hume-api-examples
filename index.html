Why lume?

Lume helps you question stressful thoughts and investigate beliefs that cause suffering in your life. Through this process, you often discover that your problems come from your thinking, not from the world around you. Lume enables you to resolve long-standing issues, improve relationships, and experience more joy and peace in your daily life.
